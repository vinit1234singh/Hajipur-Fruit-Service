About Service

The Fruit Service was founded in Hajipur, Bihar, which is famous for Mango, Banana and Lichi. One of the most significant selling points for fresh fruits and vegetables is that these foodstuffs are considered by dietitians, physicians and consumers themselves to be "healthy." This is because fruits and vegetables are often high in fiber, low in calories, often contain essential nutrients such as vitamin C, potassium and folic acid, and they may be high in compounds that offer significant health benefits.
